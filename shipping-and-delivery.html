Shipping & Delivery Policy

Effective Date: June 16, 2025

At Meatify, we are committed to providing fast and reliable delivery of your orders. This Shipping & Delivery Policy outlines how and when your products will be delivered to you through our mobile apps and our website https://meatify.in.

1. Delivery Coverage

We currently offer delivery services in selected cities and serviceable pin codes. You can check availability by entering your location during checkout.

2. Shipping Charges

Shipping fees may vary based on:

  • Order value
  • Delivery location
  • Ongoing promotions or offers

Shipping charges (if any) will be clearly shown before placing your order.

3. Delivery Timeframes

Estimated delivery times will be displayed during checkout and may vary depending on:

  • Product availability
  • Your location
  • Weather or traffic conditions

4. Same-Day & Scheduled Delivery

We offer same-day and scheduled delivery for selected items and areas. Availability and timing options will appear at checkout.

5. Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking link via email, SMS, or app notification. You can also track orders from the "My Orders" section in the Meatify app.

6. Delivery Attempts

Our delivery partner will make up to 2 attempts to deliver your order. If unsuccessful, your order may be canceled or rescheduled as per our policy.

7. Packaging

All items are packaged securely to ensure freshness, hygiene, and protection during transit. For perishable items, cold-chain logistics are used where necessary.

8. Delays and Force Majeure

While we strive for timely delivery, unexpected delays may occur due to natural disasters, weather conditions, lockdowns, or strikes. Meatify shall not be liable for such delays beyond our control.
