Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 16, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Meatify ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our:

  • Mobile applications ("Apps")
  • Website: https://meatify.in
  • Related services (collectively, the "Services")

By accessing or using our Services, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you disagree, please refrain from using our Services.

2. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

When you use our Services, we may collect:

  • Contact Details: Name, email, phone number, delivery address
  • Payment Data: Transaction history (processed via secure third-party gateways; we do not store card details)
  • Account Credentials: Username, password (hashed)

b. Technical & Usage Data

  • Device Information: IP address, device model, OS version
  • Logs: App crashes, browsing activity, timestamps
  • Location: Precise/GPS or approximate (with your permission)

c. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies (e.g., pixels, SDKs) to:

  • Authenticate users
  • Analyze trends (e.g., via Google Analytics)
  • Personalize ads (opt-out available)

3. How We Use Your Information

We process your data to:

  • Process orders and deliver products
  • Respond to inquiries and improve customer support
  • Send transactional emails (e.g., order confirmations) and marketing communications (opt-out anytime)
  • Prevent fraud and comply with legal obligations
  • Enhance Services via analytics (e.g., feature usage)

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We may share information with:

  • Vendors/Partners: Delivery personnel, payment processors (Razorpay, etc.)
  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting, analytics tools, marketing platforms
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law or to protect our rights

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement:

  • Encryption (SSL/TLS for data transmission)
  • Access controls and regular security audits
  • PCI-DSS compliance for payment data

Despite these measures, no system is 100% secure. Please notify us immediately at security@meatify.in if you suspect unauthorized access.

6. Your Rights

Depending on applicable laws, you may:

  • Access, correct, or delete your data
  • Withdraw consent (e.g., location tracking)
  • Request data portability
  • Lodge complaints with regulatory authorities

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@meatify.in.

7. Retention Period

We retain your data only as long as necessary for:

  • Order fulfillment and legal compliance (e.g., tax records)
  • Legitimate business needs (e.g., fraud prevention)

8. International Transfers

Data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure adequate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) where required.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our Services are not intended for users under 18 years. We do not knowingly collect children’s data.

10. Policy Updates

We may modify this policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with an updated effective date. Continued use of Services constitutes acceptance.
