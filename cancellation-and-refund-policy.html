Cancellation and Refund Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2025

This Cancellation and Refund Policy outlines the terms under which you may cancel an order and request a refund through the Meatify mobile app or website (meatify.in).

1. Order Cancellation

  • You may cancel an order within 10 minutes of placing it, provided the order has not already been processed or dispatched.
  • Once the order has moved to the "Processing" or "Out for Delivery" stage, it cannot be canceled.
  • Cancellation requests must be made through the app under the “My Orders” section or by contacting support@meatify.in.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are only issued under the following conditions:

  • The item received was damaged, spoiled, or of poor quality (photo evidence may be required).
  • The item delivered was incorrect or missing.
  • The order was canceled within the allowable time window.

3. Non-Refundable Items

Due to the perishable nature of fresh meat products, we do not offer refunds for the following:

  • Change of mind after the order is processed or delivered.
  • Delayed delivery due to factors outside our control (traffic, weather, etc.).
  • Incorrect delivery address provided by the user.

4. Refund Process

Once your refund request is approved:

  • Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days.
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used at checkout.
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the refund has been initiated.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about your order or our refund process, please contact our support team at:

Email: support@meatify.in
Customer Support Hours: 9 AM – 9 PM (IST), Monday to Sunday
